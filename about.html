About Carla Bekker

I am a curious designer that thrives in an environment where I can tackle complex problems with an exceptional eye for detail. I truly enjoy learning new skills and methods, creative or technical. My belief in good and honest design has been instilled in me from a very young age. I have a diverse background that has taught me many cross disciplinary skills. I work well in a team or as a team leader. My motivation for great design is to understand a problem thoroughly and learning from the experience that I can later apply in new projects.

I studied industrial design at Cape Peninsula University of Technology and have since then done a few small projects on the side and am looking to work with people on many more projects where I can learn and grow as an industrial designer.
